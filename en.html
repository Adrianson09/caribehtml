About Us

We develop and manage superior standard hotel investments sustainably, profitably, and with a high-level team.

Nuestra visión es ser el desarrollador líder a nivel regional de las mejores y más prestigiosas marcas de cadenas hoteleras.

Corporate Profile:

We develop and manage superior standard hotel investments sustainably, profitably, and with a high-level team. Globalization has proven to be a challenge for Latin America and the Caribbean. This phenomenon signifies an increase in trade in the region and the rest of the world.

As a result, there is a demand for high-quality products and services. This is especially reflected in the challenge of providing hotel infrastructure that can adapt to the profile of each traveler.

Our Strategy

Our Caribbean Hospitality Strategy was born with the objective of developing hotels that are managed by prestigious hotel brands. Our business process begins by selecting strategic locations to build spaces that are designed and tailored to meet the needs of the traveler.

Due to its reputation as a prestigious hotel developer, renowned international financial institutions have partnered with Caribe Hospitality by signing master financing agreements. In 2007, with the International Finance Corporation (IFC), and in 2009, with the Inter-American Development Bank (IDB). As a result of these relationships, new growth opportunities have been realized.

Our Sustainability Policy

"Caribe Hospitality is committed to developing hotel assets that operate profitably in accordance with legal, financial, environmental, energy, and other applicable requirements. We are committed to the continuous improvement of our business processes, environmental and energy performance, and our Sustainability and Energy Management Systems. Caribe Hospitality specifically commits to:

  • Protecting the environment and preventing pollution.
  • Establishing environmental and energy objectives and targets for the SGS and SGE, as well as their respective review for improving environmental and energy performance.
  • Ensuring the availability of up-to-date information and necessary resources to meet the objectives and targets.
  • Supporting the activities of designing and acquiring energy-efficient products, processes, facilities, equipment, and services that consider and impact environmental and energy performance."

    • Scope of Sustainability Management System
